Library & Reading Centre

Our well-stocked library provides students with a vast collection of textbooks, reference books, fiction, and digital resources in a peaceful, dedicated reading environment.

  • 3,000+ books in Nepali and English
  • Dedicated quiet reading room
  • Weekly reading programme for all classes
  • Reference section for SEE preparation
🔬Fully Equipped

Science Laboratory

Our integrated science lab enables students to conduct Physics, Chemistry, and Biology experiments, making learning practical, engaging, and memorable.

  • Physics, Chemistry & Biology stations
  • Modern microscopes and equipment
  • Safety gear and first aid available
  • Weekly practical sessions per class

  • 30+ updated desktop computers

  • MS Office, programming & typing tools

Smart Campus

Smart & Eco-Friendly Campus

We are committed to building a modern, sustainable learning environment for future generations

Solar Energy System

The entire campus runs on solar power supplemented by grid backup, ensuring zero disruption to learning and reducing our carbon footprint.

Clean Water Supply

Filtered drinking water stations throughout campus plus a water tank reserve ensuring uninterrupted safe water supply year-round.

CCTV Security

24/7 CCTV coverage of all campus areas, controlled entry gates, and trained security personnel ensure student safety at all times.

Internet Connectivity

High-speed internet in the computer lab and library. Wi-Fi available for staff and supervised student access for research.

Waste Management

Segregated waste bins across campus, weekly clean-up drives, and a composting programme teaching students responsible waste habits.

Fire Safety

Fire extinguishers in all buildings, clearly marked emergency exits, and annual fire safety drills for all students and staff.
