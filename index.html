About Our School

A Legacy of Learning &
Excellence in Nepal

Shree Pingla Devi Secondary School has been a cornerstone of quality education in our community for over two decades. We offer holistic education that blends academic rigour with character development, preparing students for the challenges of a modern world.

Our institution uniquely combines a nurturing Boarding School (Classes 1–5) providing round-the-clock care and learning, with an excellent Government School (Classes 6–10) that upholds the national curriculum standards while maintaining exceptional quality.

Our History

School History Timeline

A journey of growth, achievement, and unwavering commitment to educational excellence

1998 B.S.
Foundation of Shree Pingla Devi School

The school was established by the local community with a vision to bring quality education to rural Nepal, starting with Classes 1–5.

2005 B.S.
Boarding Facility Established

The boarding wing was inaugurated, providing residential facilities for students from distant villages, making quality education truly accessible.

2010 B.S.
Government Affiliation – Classes 6–10

The school received full government affiliation to run secondary classes up to Class 10, expanding its reach and academic offerings.

2015 B.S.
Science Lab & Computer Lab Inaugurated

A modern science laboratory and fully equipped computer lab were established, providing students with hands-on learning opportunities.

2019 B.S.
District Excellence Award

Recognized by the district education office for outstanding academic performance, achieving 98% pass rate in the national SEE examinations.

2082 B.S.
Digital Transformation & Expansion

Launch of e-learning systems, digital classrooms, solar power installation, and expansion of the boarding facility to accommodate more students.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions from parents and prospective students

Admission for Classes 1–5 (Boarding) requires a completed application form, the student's birth certificate, previous academic records (if applicable), and an interaction session with the student and parents. A spot assessment may be conducted. Please visit our school office or fill out the contact form below to initiate the process.

The boarding fee covers accommodation, three nutritious meals per day, evening study supervision, basic medical care, and all school facilities. Exact fee structures vary by class and are provided during the admission consultation. Scholarship opportunities are available for deserving students.

Yes. Shree Pingla Devi Secondary School (Classes 6–10) is fully affiliated with the Government of Nepal under the national curriculum framework. Students appear for the national SEE (Secondary Education Examination) and receive official government certificates.

Yes, we offer scholarship programmes for both meritorious students and those from economically disadvantaged backgrounds. Applications are reviewed on a case-by-case basis. Please contact the school office for scholarship criteria and application procedures.

Boarding students are encouraged to stay connected with family through regular supervised phone calls and designated visiting days. Parents are also updated through periodic progress reports, WhatsApp notices, and formal parent-teacher meetings each term.

We offer a rich variety of co-curricular activities including football, cricket, volleyball, athletics, music, dance, drama, art & craft, science club, debate society, and community service projects. These activities are integrated into the school timetable to ensure all students can participate.
