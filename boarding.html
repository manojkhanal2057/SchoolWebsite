A Home Away from Home

Our boarding programme is designed to give young children a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment where they can grow holistically — intellectually, emotionally, and socially.

Children living away from home for the first time receive exceptional care from our trained house parents who are committed to making every student feel loved, secure, and supported. We follow a carefully structured daily routine that balances academics, recreation, meals, and rest — ensuring every child thrives.

Amenities

Boarding Facilities

Everything our residential students need — from comfortable dormitories to a full range of learning and recreation spaces

Dormitories

Spacious, well-ventilated sleeping quarters with individual storage, proper bedding, and age-group separation.

Dining Hall

Clean, well-maintained dining area serving three nutritious meals and snacks prepared by trained cooks.

Study Room

Quiet, well-lit study hall for evening homework sessions, supervised by teachers from 6–8 PM daily.

Play Area

Designated outdoor play and sports area with scheduled recreation time to ensure physical fitness and fun.

Bathrooms

Clean, separate bathroom blocks for boys and girls with hot water availability and regular maintenance.

Medical Room

On-premises medical room with trained staff and first aid supplies, connected to local health services.

Safe Environment

24/7 security with CCTV monitoring, controlled entry/exit, and trained residential staff on night duty.

Laundry Service

Weekly laundry facility managed by hostel staff, ensuring students always have clean clothes and uniforms.

Routine

A Balanced Daily Schedule

Our carefully crafted daily routine ensures every boarding student gets the perfect balance of academics, recreation, nutrition, and rest — building healthy habits for life.

Why Structure Matters

A predictable, structured routine helps young children feel safe and confident. It reduces anxiety, improves focus, builds self-discipline, and creates positive habits that serve them well throughout their academic journey.

5:30 AM
Morning Wake-Up

Students wake up, freshen up, and assemble for morning prayers and yoga

6:00 AM
Morning Exercise & Yoga

Light physical activity and breathing exercises to start the day with energy

7:00 AM
Breakfast

Nutritious morning meal in the dining hall before getting ready for school

8:00 AM
School Classes Begin

Regular school day with engaging lessons, activities, and learning sessions

12:30 PM
Lunch Break

Hot nutritious lunch followed by a short rest period for younger students

4:00 PM
School Ends – Snack & Play

Evening snack followed by free outdoor play, sports, and creative activities

6:00 PM
Evening Study Hour

Supervised homework and revision session with teacher assistance available

8:00 PM
Dinner

Evening meal followed by free time, storytelling, and wind-down activities

9:30 PM
Lights Out – Rest Time

All students retire for a full night's rest to ensure alertness the next day
