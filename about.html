Who We Are

A Legacy of Learning &
Excellence in Nepal

Shree Pingla Devi Secondary School has been a cornerstone of quality education in Achham, Nepal for over two decades. We offer holistic education that blends academic rigour with character development.

Our institution uniquely combines a nurturing Boarding School (Classes 1–5) providing round-the-clock care, with an excellent Government School (Classes 6–10) that upholds national curriculum standards while delivering exceptional quality education for every student.

"Dear Students, Parents, and Valued Members of our School Community,

It is with immense pride and joy that I welcome you to Shree Pingla Devi Secondary School. Our institution has been a beacon of learning and character development in Nepal for over two decades, and I am honoured to lead this remarkable community of dedicated educators and bright young minds.

Our Purpose

Mission, Vision & Values

The guiding principles that shape every decision, lesson, and moment at our school

Our Mission

To provide accessible, high-quality education that empowers every student with knowledge, skills, and values — preparing them to become responsible citizens and lifelong learners.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution in Nepal, recognised for academic excellence, innovative teaching, and the holistic development of students who positively impact their nation.

Core Values

Integrity, respect, and compassion form our bedrock. We celebrate diversity, encourage curiosity, reward perseverance, and believe every child deserves the opportunity to thrive.

Our Journey

School History Timeline

Over two decades of growth, achievement, and unwavering commitment to educational excellence

1998 B.S.
Foundation of Shree Pingla Devi School

Established by the local community to bring quality education to Achham, Nepal, starting with Classes 1–5 and a founding team of 8 dedicated teachers.

2005 B.S.
Boarding Facility Established

The residential boarding wing was inaugurated, providing safe, supervised accommodation for students from distant villages across the district.

2010 B.S.
Government Affiliation – Classes 6–10

Received full government affiliation to run secondary classes up to Class 10, expanding academic offerings and recognition under Nepal's national framework.

2015 B.S.
Science Lab & Computer Lab Inaugurated

A state-of-the-art science laboratory and computer lab were established, providing students with hands-on and digital learning opportunities.

2019 B.S.
District Excellence Award

Recognised by the district education office for outstanding academic performance — 98% SEE pass rate — and exceptional contribution to community education.

2082 B.S.
Digital Transformation & Expansion

Launch of digital classrooms, e-learning systems, solar power installation, and boarding expansion to accommodate more students from remote areas.
